# Knowledge Extraction

## Workflow

1. **Review what happened** — what was built, what was learned, what went wrong
2. **Extract decisions** — why was approach X chosen over Y?
3. **Extract gotchas** — what broke unexpectedly, what was the fix?
4. **Extract patterns** — reusable approaches discovered during the session
5. **Save to file** — `/checkpoint save knowledge` or write to `~/.pi/agent/knowledge/`
6. **Compose for future use** — `/compose create <name> <content>`
